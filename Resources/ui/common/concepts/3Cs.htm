Definition: The 3C's Model is a business model, which offers a strategic look at the factors needed for success.

  1. The Corporation What are the internal strengths of the organization? How could these be developed in order to target a market opportunity? How could the corporation become more cost effective?
  2. The Customer What does the customer want? What needs are not currently being addressed? Can customer segmentation help you to identify opportunities?
  3. The Competitors What is the competition doing? What are potential sources of differentiation, including: purchasing, design, engineering, sales and servicing.
