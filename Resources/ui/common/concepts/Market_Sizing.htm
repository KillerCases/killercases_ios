Calculate the theoretical size of the defined market in monetary value.

  1. Start by identifying the key drivers e.g. population, car ownership, age
  2. Segment based on these factors
  3. Calculate volume
  4. Identify Price
  5. Calculate Market Size

e.g. How many people buy a new car tire each year?

  1. Population of US 300M
  2. Car Ownership 80%
  3. Tires can be classified as: Emergency Replacement or Regular Maintenance
  4. Emergency Replacement affects 10% of car owners
  5. Regular Maintenance affects 25% of car owners
  6. Each tire costs $150
  7. Volume = 84M
  8. Market Size = $12.6B
