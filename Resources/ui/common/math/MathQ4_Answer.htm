Q2 Should the company continue with all business units?

Answer 2

  • Unit C appears not to be profitable based on our allocation of Fixed Costs
  • However, if Unit C were discontinued then Fixed Costs would be spread across the remaining business units.
  • Therefore Unit C should not be closed down, assuming Fixed Costs do not change

Q3 At what level of variable cost for business unit C does it make sense to close this business?

Answer 3

If variable costs increase to $500 then the loss on unit C will be equal to the extra fixed costs that would be charged to other business units

This would be a 25% increase in Variable Costs
