Q1 What is another term for "Cost of Sales"?

Answer 1

Cost of Sales may also be described as Variable Costs or Cost of Goods Sold (COGS). This includes Raw Materials and Labour.

Operating Expenses may also be described as Fixed Costs. These include Marketing, Sales, Research and Development, Payroll, Insurance, Leased Equipment and Utilities.
