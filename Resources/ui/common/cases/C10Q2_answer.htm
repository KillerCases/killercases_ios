What are the pros and cons of moving to a model with fixed rate mortgage products sold in the secondary market?

  • ChelmWest would focus on mortgage origination/lead generation
  • Mortgages would be packaged and resold on the secondary market every 6 months
  • Fixed rates would include 2 year, 5 year etc options

Answer 2

Pros

  • More mortgage origination will be possible since ChelmWest is no longer constrained to $6bn in capital.
  • Higher return on capital for ChelmWest

Cons

  • Need to hedge against risk of interest rate changes. For example, if mortgages are packaged and resold only every 6 months then there is a risk of interest rates changing within this period.
  • Need a sophisticated and experienced team to manage this process and avoid the pitfalls. For example there is a risk of over extending if buyers can't be found for portfolio.
