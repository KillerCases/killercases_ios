Szmu Pharma is interested in improving sales force effectiveness, ahead of the Asthma drug release. What would you recommend?

  • Revenue Breakdown: 25% UK, 75% USA
  • Salesforce: 6k in UK, 80k in USA
  • Visits per salesperson: 10 in UK, 15 in USA
  • Budget for other marketing activities: £4M in UK, £40M in USA

Answer 3

Basic Points

  • USA salesforce appears to be a lot less efficient than UK, so need to focus efforts here
  • For 3x revenue, USA takes 13x bigger salesforce and other marketing budget

Advanced Comments

  • Local Factors: USA has more competition and pharma marketing, so difficult to make direct comparison between countries e.g. estimate that "other marketing activities" may include direct to consumer which is not permitted in UK

  • Cost Effectiveness: Should identify range of performance in each market, cut back on unproductive activities and focus on others
